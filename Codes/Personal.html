------------------ My Hero ------------------


Just as a tree without roots is dead, a people without history or cultural roots also becomes a dead people. - Malcolm X

Malcolm X

One of the greatest Revolutionary and Human Rights Activist to to live
Malcom X dedicated his life to uplifting the black community across America through his articulated and empowering speeches.
He was however assassinated on On February 21, 1965. but his legacy lived on as he became a symbol of Black Power.


The phrase words are powerful maybe something you have heard before but to see how one person can have so much influence through just his words is a spectacle to behold. The ability to sway persons with your words doesn't come to everyone and it takes courage to speak your mind Infront of persons who do not have your best interest at heart.
Even knowing his life was in danger he choose not to cower and to stand up for what he believed in and that in itself is worthy of admiration.



------------------ Personality Test ------------------

Keirsey Temperament Sorter

Using The Keirsey Personality test I discovered my personality type is apparently that of a

Guardian

These are practical and down to earth persons who follow the rules and cooperate well with others. Persons who can have fun but duty is always the first priority.

I think this describes me well as I like to Know what exactly is expected of me and the steps I should take. I can work with other persons if needed as I try to see their point of view and to find some common ground.

There are four different types of guardian:

Supervisor
The responsible overseer who likes to take charge and normally belongs to a variety of clubs and association

Inspectors
The quite and duty oriented individual who ensure the rules are respected and very observant with those in their care

Providers
The most sociable and caring person who give their time and energy to ensure others are okay.

Protectors
The persons who take it upon themselves to guarantee the health and safety of those around them especially friends and family

The Inspector describes me the closest as I take my time to learn the rules and procedures when doing a task. I also am very adamant that rules are followed not just for my sake but to ensure the safety of others. I am not very loud about it but I will try to make sure others comply with the instructions given.
